The writing style for the book is:

- **Conversational and Straightforward**: Designed to engage readers with clear, accessible language, avoiding overly formal or robotic tones.
- **12th-Grade Reading Level**: Ensures clarity for a broad audience while maintaining professionalism.
- **Reflective of Author’s Personality**: Homage and likeness to the writers such as Ta-Nehisi Coates, James Baldwin, and Ibram X. Kendi. Infused with the author’s voice, providing relatable and approachable commentary.
- **Actionable and Practical**: Focuses on delivering solutions and insights with real-world examples and case studies.
- **Balanced for Technical and Non-Technical Readers**: Avoids jargon while explaining complex ideas in simple terms.
